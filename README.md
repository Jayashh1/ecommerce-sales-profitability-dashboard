# ecommerce-sales-profitability-dashboard

Problem Statement:
The business is experiencing steady growth in sales but inconsistent profitability across regions and product categories. The goal is to identify key drivers impacting profit and uncover opportunities for optimization.

Objective:
- Analyze sales, cost, and profit trends
- Identify high-performing and underperforming categories
- Evaluate regional performance
- Provide actionable business recommendations

Tools Used:
- Power BI
- Excel

Dataset Details:
- Order-level transactional data including:
  - Order Date
  - Customer Name
  - Region
  - Category & Sub-category
  - Sales, Cost, Profit
  - Quantity
  - Ship Mode

Dashboard Features:
- KPI Cards: Total Sales (1.3M), Cost (640K), Profit (627K)
- Time-based filtering (Order Date)
- Ship mode filtering
- Region-wise sales distribution (Map)
- Sub-category performance analysis
- Customer-level breakdown
- Quantity distribution by region
- Order trend analysis (Year & Quarter)

Key Insights:
- High sales do not always translate into high profit, indicating margin inconsistencies
- Certain sub-categories contribute significantly to revenue but have lower profitability
- Central region contributes highest quantity but requires margin evaluation
- Order volume shows seasonal fluctuations with noticeable peaks

Business Recommendations:
- Focus on high-margin sub-categories to improve profitability
- Optimize cost structure in low-margin categories
- Re-evaluate pricing or discount strategies in underperforming segments
- Improve operational efficiency in high-volume regions
- Leverage seasonal trends for targeted promotions
